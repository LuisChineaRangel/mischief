About Us

Mischief Studio is a web page focused on the video game sector, where we write articles about them, showing technical aspects. It was born back in 2019 as a personal project from a video game enthusiast and digital artist.

In addition, since the sector is our passion, we do not limit ourselves to that, we analyze the most trending games of the moment from the narrative, the social messages that they leave with the public and the emotions that they transmit to us.

Hope you enjoy every one of our posts!
