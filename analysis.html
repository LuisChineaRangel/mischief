How Dark Souls created its Own Genre

Dark Souls created a new genre known as "Soulsborne" or "Souls-like" games. It achieved this by introducing unique gameplay mechanics, including a high level of difficulty, intricate level design, and immersive storytelling. Its success has inspired many games that follow its gameplay and design philosophy.
