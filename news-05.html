A Very Fruity Success

By Juan Carlos Rey Medina Published on 2023-04-01

"Peaches" is a song by American actor and musician Jack Black, released as a single on April 7, 2023. The song was written as part of the soundtrack for the Super Mario Bros. movie, in which Black's character, Bowser, performs the piano ballad to secretly confess his love for Princess Peach. The song debuted on the US Billboard Hot 100 chart at number 83, becoming Black's first solo single to enter the chart. Concept

The musical background for Bowser was not present in the first draft of the movie. Aaron Horvath, one of the co-directors, told GameSpot that he wanted a musical Bowser, but planned a more metal-themed song in the vein of Tenacious D. After realizing it would be boring for Bowser to simply say "I love Princess Peach," Horvath decided to tell the song through a ballad. The original demo was recorded with Eric Osmond on vocals. Two days later, Black completed the final recording of the song with an original piano track from his pianist.
